# AdaTyping — station design (the 17)

The formula every station keeps: **≤90 seconds**, 3–5 mini-stages, glowing-finger hand
diagram, gentle shake on a wrong key (never a buzzer), tones that climb the major scale,
chord + reward burst at the end, 🌲 escape hatch top-left. No timers, no scores, no reading.

Already built: name lesson (`name.html`), j/k glade (`jk.html`), d/f glade (`df.html`).
Coverage so far: f d j k + Shift. Missing: s l (ring), a ; (pinky), g h (index reach),
both-hands play, first CVC words.

## Arc 1 — new fingers, one at a time

| # | Station | Keys | File |
|---|---------|------|------|
| 1 | 💎 **Earring Studio** | `l` (right ring) | `earrings.html` |
| 2 | 🐌 **Snail Spa** | `s` (left ring) | `snail.html` |
| 3 | 🐜 **Ant Parade** | `a` (left pinky) | `ants.html` |
| 4 | 😉 **The Wink** | `;` (right pinky) | `wink.html` |
| 5 | 🦋 **Butterfly Wings** | `a` ↔ `;` (both pinkies) | `butterfly.html` |

1. **Earring Studio** — a golden jewellery bar. Every `l` hangs another dangly earring
   (hoop, star, strawberry, butterfly…) with a *ching* one note higher. Finale: the bar
   sparkles and jingles like a wind chime.
2. **Snail Spa** — a snail in a bubble bath. Each `s` squirts a swirl of coloured shampoo
   onto its shell: plain → swirly → full rainbow gloss. Finale: eye-stalk wiggle + hand mirror.
3. **Ant Parade** — smallest finger, smallest animal. Each `a` marches on another ant in a
   tiny hat. Eight ants = marching band; last ant does a backflip.
4. **The Wink** — the `;` key IS a winking face. Each `;` makes the big face wink (*boop*);
   bunny, fox and deer pop in wanting winks too. Finale: a Mexican wave of winks.
5. **Butterfly Wings** — `a` flaps the left wing, `;` the right; alternating presses fly it
   up through falling petals. Lands on a flower crown… on a hedgehog.

6. **Hair Braider** — pony tail in four strands colour-coded to fingers; rolling
   `a-s-d-f`/`f-d-s-a` weaves one crossing per press. Bow pops on at the end.
7. **Juggling Fox** — circus fox; each press of a roll tosses another ball; a full roll
   keeps four in the air. Misses just bobble. Behind-the-back finale.
8. **Stretchy Giraffes** — `g` stretches the left giraffe's neck to the middle, `h` the
   right — the index-finger reach. Noses meet: heart, blush, giggle.
9. **Tightrope** — tutu elephant, one step per press, left key = left foot; each stage a
   different mirror pair so every finger pair gets a turn. Wobble on wrong key. Roses thrown.

## Arc 3 — the whole home row as a playground

| # | Station | Keys | File |
|---|---------|------|------|
| 10 | 🎹 **Piano Paws** | all 8 as notes | `piano.html` |
| 11 | 🪩 **Dance Party** | mirror pairs = moves | `dance.html` |
| 12 | 🫧 **Bubble Fashion Show** | random singles | `fashion.html` |
| 13 | 🧸 **Squishy Factory** | triple-presses | `factory.html` |
| 14 | 🌙 **Firefly Jars** | gentle timing | `fireflies.html` |

10. **Piano Paws** — home row = piano (`a`=C … `;`=C′). A kitten walks the keys lighting
    one at a time; she follows and has played *Twinkle Twinkle* without reading a note.
    Plays back at full speed after. **The tune station.**
11. **Dance Party** — disco possum: `f/j` arms, `d/k` hips, `s/l` kicks, `a/;` jazz hands.
    Build a 6-move routine, then it replays with music and mirror ball.
12. **Bubble Fashion Show** — bubbles drift up holding glowing letters; popping one drops a
    fashion item onto the capybara model. Catwalk finale with camera flashes.
13. **Squishy Factory** — conveyor squishy shows a letter; press it 3×: inflate → paint →
    face. Five on the shelf, then the whole shelf tips into the squishy-physics pile.
14. **Firefly Jars** — dusk forest; fireflies slowly glow with a letter, press while bright
    to jar them. No misses possible. Full jar becomes a lantern. The wind-down station.

## Arc 4 — patterns and first words

| # | Station | Keys | File |
|---|---------|------|------|
| 15 | 🦔 **Hedgehog Hairstyles** | copy 2–4 key patterns | `hedgehog.html` |
| 16 | 🐣 **Word Nest** | first CVC words | `words.html` |
| 17 | 🎪 **Circus Grand Finale** | everything | `circus.html` |

15. **Hedgehog Hairstyles** — Simon-style: each home key = a colour spike; copy the lit
    pattern (2 → 3 → 4). Rainbow mohawk grows. Mirror-gasp-sparkle finale.
16. **Word Nest** — mama bird shows a **picture** and **says the word**; letters appear one
    at a time with finger cues. Home-row/reach words only: **dad, sad, lad, fad, had, gal,
    jag**. Typing words before she can read them.
17. **Circus Grand Finale** — 30-second medley of cameos from every station, then fireworks
    spell **A D A** and every animal bows. The reward for visiting everywhere.

## Forest layout

Four clearings along a winding path (Arc 1 nearest; circus tent visible in the distance as
the goal). Each portal colour-matched to its station with its greeter animal — she navigates
by animal, never by text.
